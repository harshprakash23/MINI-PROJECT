Flags of South Asian Countries

India

The Indian flag, known as the Tiranga, features three horizontal bands of saffron, white, and green with a navy blue Ashoka Chakra in the center.

Pakistan

Pakistan's flag consists of a dark green field with a white vertical band and a white crescent moon and star at the center.

Afghanistan

The flag of Afghanistan features three vertical stripes of black, red, and green, with the national emblem in the center.

Bhutan

Bhutan's flag is divided diagonally into two halves, yellow and orange, with a white dragon across the middle.

Bangladesh

The flag of Bangladesh has a dark green background with a red circle slightly off-center towards the hoist.

Nepal

Nepal's unique non-rectangular flag consists of two stacked triangles in crimson red with symbols of the sun and moon.

Sri Lanka

The Sri Lankan flag, also known as the Lion Flag, features a gold lion holding a kastane sword against a maroon background with four bo leaves in each corner and two stripes of green and saffron.

Iran

Iran's flag consists of three horizontal bands of green, white, and red, with the national emblem and Takbir written in white Kufic script in the central band.
